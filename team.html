The Dogwood Homes Team

At Dogwood Homes, we're proud to bring you a dedicated team of staff and specialty craftsmen who work together to make your home transformation seamless and even enjoyable. Guided by Gareth and our experienced project and production management team, we have a glowing track record of delivering the expertise you need for everything every size remodle project.

Dedication to Service & Results
Owner | Project Manager

Gareth Hughes

Gareth started Dogwood Homes in 2016 after spending over 20 years working for The Hatcher Agency and learning how to deliver "Outrageous Service." During this time, Gareth and his wife, Cristie, renovated and flipped several historical homes in Little Rock and North Little Rock. He realized that he had a passion for remodeling homes and working with people. So when starting Dogwood Homes, he wanted to bring that same outrageous service model he learned at The Hatcher Agency to the construction industry.

Dogwood Homes has grown into a company with two project managers, one full-time designer, two part-time designers, and over 50 dedicated sub-contractors. Since 2016, Dogwood Homes has remodeled over 400 properties in the Central Arkansas area. Gareth is very involved with the local community having served on the board for Children's Protection Center and Big Brother Big Sisters in addition to sponsoring/donating to many other local organizations and schools.

Interior Designer

Sarah Jaynes

Sarah has a passion for interior design that began back in 7th grade when she spent the day shadowing an interior designer for career day. Over 30 years later, she still loves the work that allows her to help clients create a beautiful space that they can call home.

Sarah graduated from the University of Arkansas in 2002 with a Bachelor's degree in Interior Design. After several years working with and learning from well-established design companies and home builders in Northwest Arkansas, she started her own business, InsideOut Designs. She offers a wide range of design services - from paint color consulting to fabric and furniture selections, all the way to full-scope new home construction. Her business is based out of Fayetteville where her personal and professional life continues to grow and thrive.

Sarah and Cristie are lifelong friends and have teamed up throughout the years on many design projects. They bring their individual skill sets and strengths, blending them together for a well executed design aesthetic.

our specialty contractors

At Dogwood Homes, we understand that delivering the best results for your home remodeling or custom home construction requires specialized expertise.

That's why we collaborate with a trusted network of highly skilled professionals, including architects, designers, electricians, plumbers, and tile specialists. These experts are carefully selected to bring their incredible knowledge and professionalism to every project.

Our specialty contractors are registered, insured, and licensed, ensuring every detail is handled with precision and care. To maintain the highest standards, our team closely supervises all specialty contractors and coordinates their work seamlessly with our staff.

Many of these experts have partnered with us for years, sharing our commitment to quality and delivering exceptional results on every project, from residential renovations to luxury home building.
